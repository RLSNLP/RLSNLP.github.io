Ruyi Gan, **Renliang Sun**, Ziwei Wu and 8 other authors

We proposed Ziya2, a 13B parameter model built on LLaMA2 with data-centric optimization, continued pre-training on 700B tokens. Ziya2 outperforms GPT-3.5-turbo on Chinese benchmarks.

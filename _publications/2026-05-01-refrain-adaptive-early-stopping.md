**Renliang Sun***, Wei Cheng*, Dawei Li*, Haifeng Chen, Wei Wang

We present REFRAIN, a training-free framework that adaptively determines when to stop reasoning to mitigate overthinking. It integrates a two-stage stop discriminator and a sliding-window Upper Confidence Bound multi-armed bandit controller. On average, REFRAIN reduces token usage by 20-55% while maintaining or improving accuracy.

Mengyuan Liu*, He Cao*, **Renliang Sun***, Rui Wang, Yu Li, Jiaxing Zhang

We proposed a two-stage automatic data generation framework to create CAPE, a Chinese dataset based on Cognitive Appraisal theory. Models trained on CAPE deliver responses more aligned with human emotional expressions.

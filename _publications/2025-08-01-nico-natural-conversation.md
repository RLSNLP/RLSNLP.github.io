**Renliang Sun**, Shiping Yang, Mengyuan Liu, Rui Wang, Junqing He, Jiaxing Zhang

We construct a multi-turn natural interactive conversation dataset in Chinese named NICO, covering 20 daily topics and 5 interaction types. NICO helps LLMs generate more human-like responses.
